Watergun Reel & Members' Bios

Watergun is a creative collective specialised in directing and producing music videos, branded content and film.

We create videos aimed to inspire, innovate and engage, working together with brands, artists and agencies from the media and entertainment industries.

Watergunners

  • Rafael Pavon

    Director

    @watergunner

    Rafael worked for 6 years as an art director in advertising, developing his creative and visual skills in agencies such as Young & Rubicam, Delete London and Remo until 2006 when he moved to London.

    He graduated with an MA in Communication Design and Digital Media at Central Saint Martins College of Art in 2008 and decided join forces with Ricardo, Lucas and Eric and start Watergun.
