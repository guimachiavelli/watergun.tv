Unicorn Kid

Insert coin to join the Unicorn Kid on this 32-bit sleepwalking journey through Chinatown to find the polygon girl of his dreams.

“Unicorn Kid can get a kind of WTF reaction from a lot of people. These people don’t understand fun. Yes, it’s camp rave music and the hypercolour visuals are clearly the work of someone who has never rationed their sugar intake but that is why it’s brilliant.”
Mint Magazine

“You know that type of morning where you wake up wrapped around your Japanese Dakimakura Love Pillow, bathed in pools of rave lights and then wander out into an anonymous metropolis while incorporeal hands claw at your face and reality is repeatedly punctured by incursions of flashing psychedelia and translucent avatars of video game characters? You don’t? Then you guys have clearly never eaten Long John Silver’s just before going to bed… Part Enter The Void, part Scott Pilgrim, part Hype Williams, and all Unicorn Kid. U need this video.”
Mishka NYC
